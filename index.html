Ball Sort: Color Puzzle — Calm Your Mind by Sorting Balls!

Dive into a colorful, mesmerizing world of logic, relaxation, and satisfaction with Ball Sort: Color Puzzle — a mobile puzzle game that combines simplicity, aesthetics, and mental stimulation.

Your objective is crystal clear yet deeply engaging: sort the colored balls into tubes so that each tube contains balls of only one color. At first, the challenge may seem easy — but as you progress progress, the game surprises you with increasingly complex setups and unexpected combinations. Each level presents a new challenge for your mind. You'll need to think several steps ahead, remain calm, and find optimal sorting strategies while maintaining balance. This harmony is the heart of the game. Ball Sort is not just a time-killer — it's a tool for sharpening focus, patience, and logical thinking. Designed with user experience in mind, the controls are so intuitive that even a child can play. The visual style is soft and pleasant, while the smooth ball animations enhance the overall enjoyment. Gentle background music and sound effects create a meditative atmosphere — you’re not just playing, you’re relaxing and entering a state of flow.

❓ Is the game free?
Yes, Ball Sort: Color Puzzle is completely free. All core features are available without payment. The game includes in-game ads that help support the project. However, you can remove ads via a one-time in-app purchase.
❓ Do I need an internet connection to play?
No, an internet connection is not required. Ball Sort works fully offline. You can play anywhere — on the subway, on a plane, or on vacation without Wi-Fi. Your data is saved locally on your device.
❓ Is there a difficulty level in the game?
The difficulty increases dynamically. Early levels are simple, helping you understand the mechanics. As you progress, the puzzles become more complex and strategic, requiring planning and patience.
❓ What is the recommended age for the game?
The recommended age is 4 and up. The game contains no violence, has no inappropriate content, and encourages logical thinking — making it suitable for both children and adults.
❓ Can I play on a tablet?
Yes, absolutely. The game is optimized for various screen sizes — from smartphones to tablets, and even emulators on desktop. The interface scales automatically.
❓ Are there in-app purchases?
? Yes, but they’re optional. You can purchase ad removal and extra hints. Everything else is fully available for free and without restrictions.
❓ What devices are supported?
Supported devices include Android (version 5.0 and above) and iOS (version 12.0 and above). You can download the game from Google Play or the App Store. Links are provided in the sections above.
❓ Can I reset my progress and start over?
Yes, you can reset your progress in the game settings. Multiple save slots are also available if you want to try different strategies or let a child play on your device.
❓ Is there music/sound, and can I turn it off?
Yes, the game includes pleasant background music and sound effects. If you prefer to play in silence, you can easily turn off the sound in the settings menu.
❓ What should I do if the game won’t start or crashes?
Try restarting your device and ensuring that the latest version of the game is installed. If the issue persists, please contact our support team:
📩 support@workstep.agency
We’ll do our best to assist you as quickly as possible.
❓ Can I play if I have color blindness?
Yes. The ball colors are chosen with contrast in mind, and we test the game for accessibility. We’re also planning to introduce additional colorblind modes in upcoming updates.
❓ Is the game suitable for relaxation?
Absolutely. Many players report that Ball Sort helps them unwind, regain focus, de-stress, and even fall asleep. The game is designed to be pressure-free — just a light mental challenge and the joy of sorting.
