Ball Sort: Color Puzzle

Relaxing yet challenging brain game!

Ball Sort: Color Puzzle — Calm Your Mind by Sorting Balls!

Dive into a colorful, mesmerizing world of logic, relaxation, and satisfaction with Ball Sort: Color Puzzle — a mobile puzzle game that combines simplicity, aesthetics, and mental stimulation.

Your objective is crystal clear yet deeply engaging: sort the colored balls into tubes so that each tube contains balls of only one color. At first, the challenge may seem easy — but as you progress progress, the game surprises you with increasingly complex setups and unexpected combinations. Each level presents a new challenge for your mind. You'll need to think several steps ahead, remain calm, and find optimal sorting strategies while maintaining balance. This harmony is the heart of the game. Ball Sort is not just a time-killer — it's a tool for sharpening focus, patience, and logical thinking. Designed with user experience in mind, the controls are so intuitive that even a child can play. The visual style is soft and pleasant, while the smooth ball animations enhance the overall enjoyment. Gentle background music and sound effects create a meditative atmosphere — you’re not just playing, you’re relaxing and entering a state of flow.

Ball Sort:

Color Puzzle is your ideal companion — whether you're traveling, relaxing at home, winding down before sleep, or taking a break at work. It requires no internet connection, doesn’t rush you, and avoids intrusive ads — just you, your thoughts, and the colorful balls waiting to be sorted. Join millions of players around the world and discover the joy of solving complex problems with simple moves. Ball Sort is more than just a game — it’s a moment of peace.
